Lasagna Recipe

Description

This recipe for lasagna is one that has been tried and true throughout my family for a few generations. I believe you and your family will absolutley enjoy this dish!

Throughout this recipe, you will see the tender love and dedication towards cooking that my family has poured into their works. LOL

  • 1lb Italian Sausage
  • 3/4lb Ground Beef
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 1/2 cup cheese (Dealer's Choice)
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 8"x13" baking pan
  • aluminium foil

Steps

  1. Cook Meats
  2. Cook noodles for 8-10 min. Then Drain
  3. Add 1/2 cup ricotta cheese to noodles
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  5. Get out your pan and line with foil
  6. Assemble 6 noodles in pan for your base layer
  7. Pour in meat
  8. Arrange the other 6 noodles to form your next layer
  9. Top with the rest of your meat and cheese
  10. Bake in your preheated oven for 25 min. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 min. Cool for 15 min before eating.
